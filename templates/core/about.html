Mission Statement

The purpose of Tyche is simple. Improve financial literacy in the UK, especially among young adults. Many young people feel anxious about money and finance, and this is rising. They want better, higher quality financial education especially about mortgages, pensions, loans, credit cards, budgeting, debt management and taxes. Over half receive a financial education from their parents - but what if these ideas are not current or helpful in the current world? We want to deliver generally timeless financial advice for the UK, that avoids referring to specific products and instead focuses on ideas that stand the test of time. Our end goal is to improve people's quality of life, especially during the current difficult economic climate.

Please note that this site provides information, not advice. Tyche is not a regulated entity and cannot be held responsible if you act on the information contained within.
